We turn your ideas into printed & personalized gifts

Prabha Graphics is a Lucknow-based print & gifting studio focused on high-quality T-shirts, mugs, corporate gifts and personalized products for individuals, startups and leading brands.

Our story

From a small shop to a trusted printing partner

We started with a simple goal — make customized printing easy, reliable and affordable for everyone. Today we serve walk-in customers, local businesses and pan-India orders with the same attention to detail.

Over the years, Prabha Graphics has worked with corporates, schools, colleges, NGOs and online creators to deliver everything from simple printed mugs to complete event merchandise kits.

Our team handles design, printing, finishing and packing in-house so you get consistent quality and committed delivery timelines — whether you order 1 piece or 1,000.

We understand that every print carries your brand and emotions, so we treat each order like our own.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Here are some quick answers. You can always call or WhatsApp us for anything specific.

Do you take single-piece personalized orders?
Yes. Mugs, cushions, photo frames, name plates and many other products can be ordered as a single piece. Some items may have a minimum quantity, which is mentioned on the product page.
Can you handle urgent or last-minute orders?
For urgent orders, contact us directly on phone or WhatsApp. If we have slot and material availability, we do our best to deliver within your timeline.
Do you deliver outside Lucknow?
Yes, we ship many products across India using courier partners. Shipping charges depend on weight, quantity and location, and are shared clearly before confirmation.
How can I get a proper quote?
You can use the Get a Quote page, the Contact Us form, or directly WhatsApp us with your requirement, quantity and sample design.
